Movie Recommendation System

A movie recommendation system or a movie recommender is an ML-based approach to filtering or predicting the users' film preferences based on their past choices and behavior. It's an advanced filtration mechanism that predicts the possible movie choices of the concerned user and their preferences towards a domain-specific item.
